Safaricom at
a glance

Safaricom has about 28 million subscribers and enjoys a subscriber market share of 71.9%. The company has a 2G/3G/4G network coverage with 95% of its subscribers on 2G, 85% on 3G and 25% are on 4G. The company has employed 5,400 people and boosts of an employee gender ratio of 50% male and 50% female.

M-PESA - which is the company’s mobile cash transfer service - has more than 26 million active users. About KSh7.5 billion is transacted monthly on M-PESA which means 43% of Kenya’s Gross Domestic Product being transacted through this platform. The company has made investments totaling to KSh 6 billion towards social impact projects.
