The story

As the leading telecommunication service provider in Kenya, Safaricom prides itself in providing a vast range of integrated communication services. We exist to transform lives and our customers are at the heart of this belief. With 28 million subscribers, a 71.9% market share in subscription in the country, Safaricom aims to give the customer more control on how they use voice, data internet, SMS, and MPESA by rolling out resources such as My Data Manager, M-Ledger, Safaricom Gurantee, just to name a few.

1TAP

Safaricom boasts of a robust network where customers can enjoy 2G, 3G and the now-new 4G network in select towns across the country. To achieve our goal in driving connectivity, we have added 4600 base stations, at a cost of Sh. 38 billion in the network expansion project this financial year. We also paid an additional USD25 million (Kshs2.5 billion) in the year to acquire 10Mhz of LTE spectrum and rolled out more than 500 new sites during the year.

RCurving a future

Safaricom saw a 43.3% free cash flow growth in the FY17. We also experienced a 14.8% service revenue growth totaling Sh. 204.1 Billion driven by MPESA and Data whereby the net income rose by 18.3% to Sh. 48.4 Billion. Safaricom presented a special dividend to its shareholders totaling 27.2 billion that will be cascaded to its shareholders at Sh. 0.97 per share.
